Bachelor of Commerce, McGill University (expected 2018). Concentration in Operations Management, Minor Statistics for Management, Minor Concentration Computer Science.

Operations Management

The study of the design, planning, control, coordination and improvement of business processes, with an emphasis on quantitative analysis and cross functional thinking.

Statistics

The study of the collection, interpretation, and analysis of data – all of which are crucial in fields ranging from finance to data science.


Computer Science
